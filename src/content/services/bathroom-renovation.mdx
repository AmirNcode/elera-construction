## Why homeowners renovate bathrooms with Elera

A bathroom is small, but the number of trades, materials, and waterproofing details packed into it is large. Get one stage wrong and you're chasing leaks five years later.

Our team has built en-suites for new-construction condos and detached homes across the GTA — under the warranty programs of Toronto's largest residential developers. The same standards apply when we renovate yours: full waterproofing membranes, properly sloped shower pans, code-compliant ventilation, and supplier-direct tile from the same vendors the developers use.

## What sets our bathrooms apart

- **Waterproofing as a non-negotiable.** Every wet wall and shower pan gets a continuous membrane. No shortcuts on the work you'll never see.
- **Tile at trade pricing.** Our supplier accounts give you access to designer tile (porcelain, marble, mosaic) at prices the retail showrooms can't quote.
- **One project lead, start to finish.** You text or email the same person from quote to final walkthrough. No call-centre handoffs.
- **2-year workmanship warranty in writing.** If something we built fails, we come back and fix it.

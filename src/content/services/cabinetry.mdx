    answer: "Stock cabinetry (e.g., IKEA) ships in fixed sizes and finishes; fast and cheap but limited. Semi-custom uses standardized boxes but lets you pick wood species, door style, finish, and modify dimensions in 1-inch increments. ## Cabinetry is where most of your budget goes

In a kitchen, cabinetry is typically 40–55% of the total project cost. In a primary bathroom, the vanity is often the most expensive single item. Choices made here cascade into every other line item — countertop dimensions, appliance fit, hardware reveal, lighting placement.

Our suppliers (the same ones who serve new-construction developers across the GTA) give you a wider range of finishes, faster lead times, and meaningfully better pricing than retail-only cabinet companies.
